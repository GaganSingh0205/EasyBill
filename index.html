Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

Can I change plans anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be prorated and reflected in your next billing cycle.
Is there a contract or long-term commitment?
No, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel anytime.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits?
Yes, we offer a 25% discount for registered non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team for more information.

Our Story

EasyBill was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make billing and invoicing effortless for businesses of all sizes. We noticed that many business owners were spending countless hours on administrative tasks instead of focusing on what they do best.

Our team of developers, designers, and finance experts came together to create a solution that combines powerful features with an intuitive interface. Today, EasyBill serves over 25,000 businesses worldwide, helping them save time and get paid faster.

We believe that managing your finances should be simple, secure, and accessible to everyone. That's why we're committed to continuous innovation and exceptional customer support.
